Did You Know that...? There are countless types of Coffee worldwide!

Explore Different Types of Coffee

  • Espresso

    A concentrated coffee made by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans.

  • Americano

    Espresso diluted with hot water, making it similar to drip coffee but with a more intense flavor.

  • Cappuccino

    Equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam. It's usually topped with cocoa powder or cinnamon.

  • Latte

    Espresso with steamed milk and a small amount of milk foam. It's creamier than a cappuccino.

  • Macchiato

    Espresso "stained" with a small amount of milk or milk foam.

  • Cortado

    Equal parts espresso and steamed milk, usually served in a small glass.

  • Irish Coffee

    Coffee mixed with Irish whiskey and sugar, topped with cream.
